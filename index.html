Location

Convenient for food, drinks, shopping, and late-night plans.

The area around Kanamachi has convenience stores, casual restaurants, bars, ramen shops, supermarkets, drugstores, and other useful facilities for travelers. It is also a convenient base for visiting Asakusa, Ueno, Akihabara, Tokyo Skytree, Tokyo Station, and other major areas.

Please read before booking: the neighborhood includes adult-oriented nighttime businesses and may have people coming and going late at night. You may hear voices, daily-life sounds, or street noise. This stay is not recommended for guests looking for a quiet residential atmosphere, a calm hotel-like environment, or a family-oriented stay.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can I check in late at night?

Yes. The rooms use self check-in, which makes late arrival easier. Please check the exact instructions after booking.

Is this good for a solo traveler?

Yes. The rooms are practical for solo travelers and business travelers who want a private, apartment-style room.

Is this good for two friends?

Yes. Room 503 has twin beds, while Rooms 402 and 405 have double beds. Please choose the room that fits your travel style.

Can women book this stay?

Yes. Eligible adult guests are welcome. However, the surrounding area includes nighttime businesses, so please review the neighborhood information before booking.

Is this recommended for families?

This stay is best suited for adult solo travelers or small adult groups. It is not recommended for guests looking for a quiet, family-oriented environment.

Is there an elevator?

No. Rooms are on the 4th and 5th floors, and the building does not have an elevator.

Can I stay for several nights?

Yes. The rooms have useful amenities such as Wi-Fi, a refrigerator, a microwave, and a washing machine.
